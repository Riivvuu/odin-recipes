Chicken Nihari

This is a simple chicken nihari recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp nihari masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are not pink.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, nihari masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 1-2 hours or until the chicken is tender and the flavors are well combined.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala on top and let it sit for 5 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken nihari!
