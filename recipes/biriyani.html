Biriyani

This is a simple biriyani recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biriyani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are not pink.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, biriyani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Stir in the yogurt, cilantro, and mint leaves. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Stir gently to combine with the chicken mixture.
  7. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala on top and let it sit for 5 minutes before serving.
  9. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your delicious homemade biriyani!
